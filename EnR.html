Star Wars Day  May 4th

Join us on May 4th for Star Wars day at the Time Tower! The whole day your favorite Star Wars characters will be throughout the facility and make sure to drop by laser tag for Rebels vs Empire fun! Make sure you come dressed as your favorite Star Wars character!

Blockheads Concert  May 26th, 7-9 PM

Join us on May 26th to attend the free of charge Blockheads music concert! It starts at 7pm and all concessions during this time will be 10 percent off! The concert will end at 9pm and The Blockheads will be at the entrance of Time Tower to sign autographs!

Father's Day  June 18th

Father's Day celebration at the Time Tower is on June 18th! Fathers and sons come in half off! There's no place better to come for some quality father and son time over competitive laser tag or air hockey matches!

Fourth of July Celebration July 4th

To celebrate our great nations freedom come to the Time Tower to enjoy a half off entry fee! When we close at 12, come outside to enjoy a complete fireworks show provided by the Time Tower to end the wonderful day of independence.

Time Tower 10th Anniversary  July 13th

We first opened back in 2006 and now we're giving back! On July 17th come in half off and enjoy free cake on the Tower! Make sure to come back because the future looks nice, trust us we've seen it.

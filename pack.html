Endless fun for any age!

The Time Tower is split into two distinct sections; The Past, for adults, and The Future, for children.

Wristband Packages

There are two different options of entrence in the form of our Alpha and Omega wristbands. Each wristband allows differnt access to the activities in both the Future and the Past sections of Time Tower. The Alpha wristband, for those looking for a more economical and base experience, will cost $10, and the Omega wristband, for those looking for a more inclusive experience, will cost $15.

Alpha wristbands, for the Future , will allow access to the arcade and bowling attractions. Omega wristbands will open the doors for the bar and billiards room.

The Alpha wristbands allow access to the Lazer Tag Arena and Bouncy Space Station, while the Omega wristband allways further access to our Pod Racing track and Shuttle VR activities.
